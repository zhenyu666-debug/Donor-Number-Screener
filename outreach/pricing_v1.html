Pricing v1 - Donor-Number Screener

All prices in RMB. Engagement work only. We do not do single-shot projects under 1 RMB; below that threshold, please use the trial tier (which is free for the first 3 customers).

Trial (free, 3 slots)

  • Input: up to 100 SMILES from your library
  • Output: Top-20 ranked by predicted DN, with 95% conformal intervals, in CSV
  • Turnaround: 3 working days
  • Cost: 0
  • Constraint: we publish your library as a case study (with anonymisation on request) and quote one customer testimonial
  • Available: 3 slots as of Q3 2026

Standard screening (3-8 RMB)

  • Input: up to 3,000 SMILES from your library (or design rules, and we generate)
  • Output:
  • Top-20 with 95% intervals (CSV)
  • Pareto front along (DN, SA) (CSV)
  • Decision rules distilled from depth-4 tree (TXT)
  • PDF report with model card, calibration plot, SHAP attributions, and Top-20 structure drawing
  • Turnaround: 5 working days
  • Cost: 3-8 RMB depending on input size and number of follow-up calls
  • One revision included (re-train on your validation data if you have any)

Annual subscription (20 RMB / year)

  • Includes: 4 standard screenings per quarter
  • Bonus: dashboard access, ad-hoc Slack/WeChat support (1 question/week)
  • Best for: mid-stage battery startups with continuous additive R&D
  • Cost: 20 RMB / year, paid quarterly (5 per quarter)

DFT-replacement project (8-15 RMB)

  • Scope: 3-month engagement, designed for labs that want a 12,000+ molecule long-list with active-learning-guided validation
  • Deliverables:
  • Custom training set (anchored to your internal DFT/MD data if available)
  • Top-50 long-list with Pareto filter
  • Top-20 final, with 95% intervals
  • Active-learning recommendation: "if you can validate 50 more, validate these 50"
  • Final handover: trained model, code, and 2-hour training session for your team
  • Turnaround: 12 weeks
  • Cost: 8-15 RMB depending on library size and validation data quality
  • NDA: standard mutual NDA signed before kick-off

What is not on this list

  • Real DFT compute: I don't run it. If you want, I can introduce you to a commercial DFT service provider at cost.
  • Cell cycling / bench work: I screen, you validate.
  • Patent writing: not offered. If you need IP protection on a discovered molecule, talk to your patent attorney.
  • SaaS multi-tenant platform: not on the roadmap. We sell services, not seat licenses.

Payment

  • Trial: 0 (no payment)
  • Standard / project: 50% on contract, 50% on delivery
  • Subscription: quarterly in advance

Cancellation

  • Trial: cancel any time
  • Standard: full refund if cancelled before pipeline starts; 50% refund if cancelled after the descriptors step
  • Subscription: cancel at end of any quarter
  • Project: pro-rata by completed milestones
